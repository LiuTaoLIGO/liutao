Welcome to Liu Tao's Website

I am a physics graduate student at the University of Florida. I am mostly interested in precision laser interferometry techniques for gravitational wave detection, particularly in the use of the new higher order Hermite-Gaussian beam shape to reduce GW detectors' thermal noise.

I have experience and great interests in both analytical/simulational and experimental aspects of laser interferometric gravitational wave observatory techniques.
